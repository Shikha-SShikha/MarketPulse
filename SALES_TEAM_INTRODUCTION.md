# MarketPulse - Your STM Publishing Intelligence Platform

## What is MarketPulse?

MarketPulse is your automated intelligence briefing system for STM (Scientific, Technical & Medical) publishing. It collects, analyzes, and summarizes market intelligence from across the industry so you don't have to.

**Think of it as:** Your personal research assistant that monitors the STM publishing industry 24/7, delivering a curated weekly brief every Monday morning.
