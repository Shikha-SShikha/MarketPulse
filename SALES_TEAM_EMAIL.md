## What It Does

**MarketPulse monitors the STM publishing industry 24/7** and delivers a curated weekly brief every Monday with:

- **Competitor intelligence:** Product launches, partnerships, service model changes (Kriyadocs, Cactus, Editage, etc.)
- **Customer pain points:** Publisher challenges and technology needs
- **Market trends:** AI/ML adoption, Open Access mandates, integrity issues, accessibility requirements
- **Actionable insights:** "Why it matters" + "Next steps" for every trend

**Goal:** Eliminate manual research and keep you ahead of the market.
